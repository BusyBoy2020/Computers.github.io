Graphics card(GPU)

Graphics Card has been given a very important job. Graphics Card handles the things you see in the monitor simply without a graphics card you will not be able to see anything on the screen, all other things will process but you will not be able to see anything on the screen. Graphics Card is also measured in Giga Bytes. Normally all the monitors require 2GB of graphics. If you have seen gamers have double monitor or triple monitor setup. They require 4GB or 6GB graphics. The most popular companies for Graphics Cards are NVIDIA and Intel. Graphic Card like Intel HD 4000 is mainly for simple word document and office meetings. Graphic Card like NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1650 super or NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1650 Ti are for light gaming and video editing. Graphic Card like NVIDIA RTX 3080 or NVIDIA RTX 3090 are for heavy gaming and advanced video editing purposes.
